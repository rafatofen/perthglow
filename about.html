About Perth Glow

Who We Are

Perth's Glow Event Specialists

Perth Glow Furniture Hire was born from a simple idea: every event deserves to be extraordinary. We specialise in LED glow furniture, dance floors, and 360 photo booths — the kind of setups that make guests stop and stare.

Based in Bassendean, we service all of Perth and surrounding areas. Over the years, we've helped light up hundreds of events — from intimate backyard birthdays to large-scale corporate functions and weddings.

Our furniture is fully wireless, rechargeable, and available in 16 stunning colours. You control the vibe — slow fade for an elegant dinner, full flash for a party atmosphere, or a single colour to match your theme.
